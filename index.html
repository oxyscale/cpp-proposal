Section 01

Where You Are Today

Since getting to know more about Complete Property Presentations over the last few weeks, the one thing that is extremely clear is that you have built an amazing business completely organically. We have a lot of respect for where you are at. What follows is our honest assessment of where we see the opportunity to make what you have built even stronger, and how our system is built to do exactly that.

Currently, the operation runs across a combination of spreadsheets, manual emails, and phone-based communication. Leads come in via calls, texts, and voicemails. Quotes are built manually and sent when there is time. Invoices are keyed into Xero by hand. Calendar entries are created manually by Tracy. Packing lists come from memory or printed notes. Property listing statuses on realestate.com are checked manually to track what has sold or gone under offer.

The team manages a high volume of inbound communication from agents. Follow-ups on open quotes can be difficult to stay on top of during busy periods. There is limited visibility on metrics like quote conversion rates, which agents are generating the most work, or how many leads come in that never get quoted.

The opportunity is not about fixing what is broken. It is about giving the business the infrastructure to handle the volume it is already doing more efficiently, and to scale from here without adding more manual work to the plate.

Complete Property Presentations is a phenomenal operation. 01
Lead Flow, Centralisation and Recovery
Right now, inbound enquiries are arriving across multiple channels - calls, texts, voicemails, and agent messages. Most leads get picked up. But the ones that do not leave no trace, and there is no way to know how many were missed, when they came in, or what they were worth.

Speed to Response
Studies consistently show that responding to a lead within 5 minutes rather than 30 minutes makes you 21 times more likely to convert. This system gives you the ability to walk out of a client meeting and have a branded quote sitting in their inbox before you have even made it back to your car. Or if quoting off a floor plan, have a fully branded quote with them from punching in a few details into our input form.
On a business doing $1 million in revenue, even a conservative 10% improvement in conversion is $100,000 per annum.
